BETTA

Betta is a digital savings wallet that allows users to save at least a dollar a day with the capability of locking the savings for a certain period.
The digital wallet seeks to solve the problem of poor saving habits amongst the unbanked population in Kenya, specifically targeting business people who earn a daily income.
Our vision is to empower millions of unbanked individuals to securely save and manage their finances, helping them build better saving habits towards achieving their financial goals.
